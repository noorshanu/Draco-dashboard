The Future of Ethereum Validators

The eHive protocol is a community inspired experiment in DeFi Crowdsourcing built on the Ethereum Network.

Contribute to the future of Ethereum today, by building the structure for tomorrow.

START EARNING BUY NOW

Preparing Today, To Build Tomorrow

In the coming months, the Ethereum network will be going through their upgrade to ETH 2.0. With this, Ethereum validators will be going live on the main net and generating ETH rewards for operators. eHive plans to open this opportunity up to the entire DeFi community through shared validators.

Stake your eHive tokens in our community validators to earn a share of the validator’s ETH rewards, as well as eHive tokens.

All ETH earned by the validator is distributed to DRAG stakes.

As the DRAG validators will be created by the DRAG token tax, all ETH generated by the validator will be distributed to staked eHive community members. This allows the eHive community to crowdsource their validators and allow users of all levels to participate in the rewards.
